How to read these numbers

  • Monthly-rest model. preclose uses the standard monthly reducing-balance method. Banks use daily-reducing balances and broken-period interest on the first partial month, so figures may differ from your statement by small (typically rupee-level, not thousand-rupee-level) amounts.
  • On-time assumption. Every EMI is assumed paid on its due date; missed payments, late fees, and penal interest are not modeled.
  • Prepayment charges are whatever you enter.

Not financial advice. preclose is an informational calculator using a standard monthly-rest reducing-balance model. Results may differ from your lender's daily-reducing figures. Verify any prepayment decision against your lender's official statement and terms before acting.
